Super Simple Salmon

This simple salmon recipe is a delicious way to prepare fresh fillets using just a few ingredients from your pantry.

  • Total Time: 15 minute (5 min prep / 10 min cook)
  • Serves: 4 servings

Ingriedients

  • 1 tablespoom Garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon Dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon Salt
  • 4 fillets Salmon
  • 2 teaspoons Butter
  • 4 wedges Lemon

Directions:

  1. Stir garlic powder, basil, and salt together in a small bowl. Rub the mixture evenly over salmon.
  2. Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Add salmon and cook until browned and flaky, about 5 minutes per side.
  3. Serve salmon with lemon wedges.
