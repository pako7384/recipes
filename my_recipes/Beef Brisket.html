Slow Cooker BBQ Beef Brisket

  • Total Time: 6 hours 10 minute (10 min prep / 6 hours cook)
  • Serves: 16 servings
INGREDIENTS QUANTITY
Beef Brisket, trimmed of fat 1
Jards dill pickle spears 2
Bottle barbeque sauce 1

    Directions:

    Step 1 Place brisket in the bottom of a slow cooker. Pour dill pickle spears and pickle juice over the beef; add barbeque sauce.

    Step 2 Cook on High until beef shreds easily with 2 forks, about 6 hours.
