Cream Cake

  • Total Time: 50 minute (20 min prep / 30 min cook)
  • Serves: 9 servings
    INGREDIENTS QUANTITY
    Nonstick cooking spray
    Large eggs 2
    White sugar 3/4 cup
    Vanilla extract 1 teaspoon
    Baking powder 2 teaspoons
    Salt 1 teaspoon
    All-purpose flour 3/2
    Heavy whipping cream 1 cup

    Directions:

    Step 1 Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x9-inch square pan with nonstick cooking spray.

    Step 2 Beat eggs in a large bowl until very thick. Mix in sugar and vanilla until well combined.

    Step 3 Sift together flour, baking powder, and salt in a separate bowl. Add flour mixture to egg mixture in 3 parts, alternating with whipping cream, mixing until combined. Pour batter into the prepared pan.

    Step 4 Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, 30 to 40 minutes.
